Privacy Policy

Somnex respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal information responsibly. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, improve our services, and maintain the security of our website. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain contact information only as long as needed to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
